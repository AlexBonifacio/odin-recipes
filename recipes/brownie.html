Brownie

Description

A classic brownie recipe starts with melting rich dark chocolate and butter together, then blending it with sugar, eggs, and a touch of vanilla to create a smooth and decadent batter. Once baked to perfection, these brownies boast a fudgy interior with a crackly, indulgent top layer, making them the ultimate chocolate treat.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate
  2. Mix the eggs
